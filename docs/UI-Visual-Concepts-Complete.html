Specific Design Improvements for Your Platform

Key Recommendations

Replace Linear Progress with Stage-Based Tracking: Your current progress indicators should show specific stages (Market Context → Competitive Intel → Customer Analysis → Strategic Assessment) rather than just percentages. This builds confidence by showing tangible progress through recognizable business analysis phases.
Add AI Confidence Indicators: Every AI-generated insight should include confidence levels (High/Medium/Low) with visual indicators. This helps users understand when to trust the AI versus when to apply their own judgment.
Implement Strategy Card Selection: Replace your current research strategy selection with visual cards showing time estimates, included features, and expected outputs. This makes the choice clearer for non-experts.
Enhance Mobile Experience: Prioritize mobile-first design with touch-friendly navigation, simplified layouts, and gesture support. This addresses the biggest competitive gap in your market.
Improve Results Presentation: Use progressive disclosure for complex analysis results. Start with key insights and conclusions, then allow users to drill down into supporting evidence and detailed analysis.
